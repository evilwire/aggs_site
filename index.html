Welcome!

Algebraic Geometry, Graduate Series is a weekly seminar run by graduate students of the Rutgers New Brunswick math department, focusing on supplementing ex-curricular interest in algebraic geometry. We usually focus on one of the cornerstone subjects in algebraic geometry per semester and present series of lectures on that subject.

We are always looking to meet anyone interested in algebraic geometry. Here's some information about us. If you would like to join, please do not hesitate to contact us.

This semester, we are meeting: Wednesdays, 6:40PM - 8:00PM in room Hill 525

This semester we are focusing on Algebraic Groups. We are working through material from James Humphreys's Linear Algebraic Groups Springer GTM. We also recommend the following as supplementary texts and go-to references.

  • Humphreys, J. E. Linear Algebraic Groups, Springer-Verlag New York, 1975 (Main Text)
  • Carter, R. Lie Algebra of Finite and Affine Type, Cambridge University Press, 2005
  • Eisenbud, D. Commutative Algebra with a View Towards Algebraic Geometry, Springer-Verlag New York, 2004
  • Hartshorne, R. Algebraic Geometry, Springer-Verlag New York, 1977
  • Humphreys, J. E. Lie Algebra and Representation Theory, Springer-Verlag New York, 1972 (Main Text)
  • Springer, T. A. Linear Algebraic Groups 2e., Birkhäuser Modern Classic, Boston, 1998
